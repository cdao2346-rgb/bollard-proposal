Project Objective

Install permanent steel bollards to protect the exterior of the building from vehicle impact. The process includes cutting asphalt, setting structural pipes in deep concrete footings, and filling cores with high-strength concrete for maximum long-term durability.

Planning & Safety Notes

CRITICAL: Underground Utilities

Before any digging begins, we must contact 811. Conduits for parking lights and water mains can run directly under asphalt. Call 48 to 72 hours before work starts.

Safety Standards (DC-82-E)

A JHA (DC-85) must be performed to address flying debris risks and heavy lifting.
